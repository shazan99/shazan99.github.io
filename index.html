Help India Breathe Fund

Help India Breathe COVID Response Fund is best described as a giving collective that enables both individuals and organizations to respond together to COVID. In 2020, we raised 220+ crores, impacting the lives of 56L+ people in India. We re-launched this campaign in March 2021, in response to the grave second wave of COVID to support gaps in healthcare and other critical needs.
